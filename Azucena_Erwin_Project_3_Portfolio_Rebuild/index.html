About Me

My name is Erwin Azucena, I identify as a Bisexual Filipino American who enjoys gaming as a hobby.

I live with my parents, my aunt, and my older sister, while two of my other older sisters reside elsewhere in California.
I have never driven a car in my life because I fear the possibility of being involved in a car accident, which could result in either my death or damage to the vehicle I would be operating.
My swimming skills are not very strong, so when I visit the pool, I tend to either sit in a distant corner or use a floatation device to avoid the risk of drowning and dies.
